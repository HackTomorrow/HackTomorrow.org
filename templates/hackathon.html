What is the HackTomorrow Hackathon?

The HackTomorrow Hackathon is a periodical celebration of innovation and creativity where hundreds of passionate young people come together to create something they believe in.

On the other hand, HackTomorrow is an organization working to give everybody, everywhere regardardless of race, region, color, status, or income to be able to contribute to the greater good and build the solutions of tomorrow, today.

Why Should You Come?

Besides the more common reasons like attending hackathons help you build your dream projects faster and you'll get free food, attending our hackathon and becoming a HackTomorrow member will open doors that will allow you to be a part of bootcamps, fairs, festivals, and other events that will help you to create things you are truly proud of.

Come be a part of something bigger.

FAQs

How Much Does It Cost?

Nothing! We believe that everyone should be able to create, irrespective of money.

Can I Attend?

Anyone 18 and above can attend, were working to allow younger members to attend.

What Should I Bring?

We reccomend bringing your laptop, chargers, sleeping bag, and whatever you'll need to be your best self.

Do I Need A Team?

Nope, it is completely optional to be a part of a team. If you want to hack alone, thats fine!

How Does Judging Work?

Our panel of judges will rate each project on a set of requirements and the highest rated projects win. The requirements will be public.

Will There Be Food?

Yup, we will provide three meals a day. Snacks, drinks, and coffee will be avalible all the time.

What Can I Make?

Anything you truly believe can make a difference. Whether its hardware, programs, or even art, we can't wait!

What If I Have More Questions?

Send us an email at contact@hacktomorrow.org
